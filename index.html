about

studio 1/1

in service of architectural design, one to one.

beliefs

Design is driven by personal values and needs.
Design materializes from a personal vision.
Design is to be in service of a user.
Design requires a strong empathy and a deep understanding of that user and its environment.
Design is therefore an intimate practice.

We are a designstudio voor architecture and design where we focus on a shared vision between the designer and the client.

practice

studio 1/1 was established in 2024 in Ghent, Belgium by a maker at heart, driven by the need to create an environment that makes her feel at home, to build furniture that brings her comfort and to craft objects that hold personal value to her.

Our mission is to design spaces and objects that hold a deep meaning for their users. We approach architecture and design with empathy and humility. We listen to your interests and needs and it is of great importance to us that we create an architecture that is fully tailored to you while highly respecting the poetry and beauty of the existing environment.

With an open mind, we explore different possibilities. We search for a solution that fits within your story, all while authenticity, sustainability, and a strong technical logic remain at the foundation of our practice. We strive to create high-quality projects with a personal value.
